The Secret.

Lianlong LI

“Do you want to talk about some things with me?” The doctor pushed his glasses up, and put the eyes to look at me.

He was so churlish and impatient. The pen has a rhythmic collision on the table.

“What?” My palms were sweating; I swear I didn’t know what does he said!

The doctor looked me again, he opened his mouth; then, he said with me by impatiently:

“No matter what can be. Such as… a secret!”

He went back to his desk again, unscrew the cap of a pen, and wrote down some words on a paper.

“A secret? What do you mean?” I don’t know how I can reply it.

I never have any secret; I living in sunshine. I always comforted myself like this.

“Everybody has some secrets. Even you living in the sunshine.” The doctor seems can feel my thoughts.

“But… There is always a place where the sunshine cannot direct-shine in this world …” The doctor stops his writing, and looked into my eyes.

"Such as?” I asked him.

“I killed a patient at my clinic.” The doctor said by indifferently.

“Really? Is here?”

“Yes, here is.” The doctor said to me, with any emotion.

“How would you do it?” I am full of doubt.

“By a pen...” the doctor tells me.

“Why would you tell me about this? Do not you afraid of telling other person about those thing?”

“No, I am very rest assured to you!”

“Why?”

Because the corpse will keep the secret!

LIANLONG LI

Dream what you want to dream; go where you want to go; be what you want to be,

Because you have only one life and one chance to do all the things you want to do.
